Prehistoric Intelligence, Made Practical.

PALEO helps build believable dinosaur behavior for survival-style games. In simple terms: it watches game visuals, understands what a dinosaur needs, and decides smart next actions.

Teams can use it to test world balance, resource flow, and behavior realism before shipping gameplay changes.

What PALEO Does

Perceive

Reads gameplay frames and detects behavior cues like danger, hunger, and nearby opportunity.

Think

Uses a per-dinosaur memory profile so each creature has continuity, preferences, and risk memory.

Act

Produces context-aware actions and explainable thought logs for debugging and balancing.

Why It Matters

Why It Matters

  • Makes NPC behavior feel less scripted and more alive.
  • Gives designers evidence when tuning maps and survival loops.
  • Adds interpretable outputs so behavior decisions can be audited.
Who It Helps

Who It Helps

  • Game studios building survival and ecosystem-driven gameplay.
  • Design teams validating resource routes and encounter difficulty.
  • Players needing assistance with heavy survival micromanagement.
