# Project-Checkin-2/2

## Details

For **Check-in 2/2**, each student should submit a concise project update covering the following items.

### 1. **Project Title**

Provide a clear, specific, and informative title for your project.

### 2. **Team Information**

Include:

- **Team name**
- **Team members**

### 3. **Problem to Solve**

Clearly describe the problem your project is addressing. Your write-up should explain:

- What the problem is
- Why it is important
- Who is affected or who would benefit from a solution

### 4. **AI Functions to Be Developed**

Describe the main AI capabilities your system will include. For example:

- Machine learning
- Search
- Computer Vision
- Neural network
- Optimization

Please be specific about what your AI system is expected to do.

### 5. **Use of Agentic AI**

Explain how **Agentic AI** will be incorporated into your project, if applicable. For example:

- Will the system plan multi-step actions?
- Will it call tools or external APIs?
- Will it reason over memory or context?
- Will it coordinate multiple AI modules?
- Will it make decisions autonomously based on feedback?

### 6. **Dataset(s)**

Describe the dataset(s) you plan to use:

- Dataset name
- Source
- Data modality (image, text, audio, video, multimodal, etc.)
- Approximate size
- Preprocessing plan

If you are collecting your own data, also include:

- Collection method
- Annotation plan
- Ethical considerations

### 7. **Evaluation Plan**

Explain how you will evaluate your project:

- What metrics will you use?
- What baseline methods or comparison methods will you use?
- How will you determine whether the project is successful?

Examples may include accuracy, F1 score, mAP,  success rate, latency, user satisfaction, robustness, or other task-specific metrics.

### 8. **Current Progress**

Summarize the progress made so far. Be specific. For example:

- Dataset downloaded or collected
- Preprocessing completed
- Baseline model implemented
- Initial experiments run
- Preliminary results obtained
- Early observations or failure cases identified

You are encouraged to include supporting evidence such as:

- Screenshots
- Sample outputs
- Training curves
- Tables or visualizations

### 9. **Next-Step Plan**

Describe your plan for the next stage of the project:

- What will be completed next?
- What experiments will be run?
- What improvements will be made?
- What timeline do you expect for the next milestones?
